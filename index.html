REPUBLIC

The Galactic Republic was the ruling government of the galaxy that existed for more than 25 thousand years, until it's reformation into the first Galactic Empire in 19 BBY. Throughout its existence, the state was commonly known as the Republic. In the post-Republic era and beyond, it was remembered as the Old Republic; it was also rarely known as the Old Galactic Republic or First Galactic Republic. At times, the term Old Order was used to describe the Republic but was not common.
